1. Power Infrastructure & Distribution
This category handles high-voltage equipment and the core backbone of a power network.

Substations & Mini Substations: Primary nodes for voltage transformation and distribution.

Transformers: Vital for stepping voltage up or down across the site.

Switch gears: Circuit protection and switching for high/medium voltage.

Power lines (electrification): Bulk utility and site-wide distribution.

Generators: Solutions for standby, backup, or off-grid power.

2. Industrial Automation & Motor Control
These services focus on the efficiency and "intelligence" of your industrial drives and motors.

Motors: The physical drivers of industrial machinery.

PLC, SCADA, SIMOCODE & VSD upgrades: The software and hardware logic used to automate and control motor speeds.

Wire-wound resistor: Used specifically for motor starting, dynamic braking, and neutral grounding.

3. Mechanical & Fluid Handling
This group focuses on the physical movement of materials and the maintenance of plant hardware.

Pumps & Valves: The core components for fluid transport and flow regulation.

Mechanical Site work: On-site fabrication, installation, and structural repairs.

4. Professional & Technical Services
These services represent your expertise and your "boots on the ground" capability.

Electrical consulting: Professional engineering, system design, and auditing.

Field services: On-site testing, commissioning, and emergency technical repairs.
