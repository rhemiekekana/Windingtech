Winding Technologies is an Electrical and Mechanical Engineering company with our head offices based in Wadeville Germiston South Africa. The company was established in 1994 as an armature winding company servicing the Mining Sector on AC/DC motors, and later expanded into Transformers, Substations, Switchgear, automation etc.. We have branches in Rustenburg, Limpopo Thohoyandou, Mpumalanga, Durban, Kimberly and Cape Town. Winding Technologies is 100% BWO (Black Woman Owned) with CIDB (Construction Industry Development Board) gradings of 8ME, 8EP.
